Game Overview

What is Minecraft?

Minecraft is an action-adventure sandbox game developed by Mojang Studios. In the game, players explore a blocky, procedurally generated, three-dimensional world with virtually infinite terrain. They may discover and extract raw materials, craft tools, and items, and build structures, earthworks, and machines.
